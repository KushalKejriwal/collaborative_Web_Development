BTech Placement Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the BTech Placement Guide. This guide will help you prepare for your campus placements and provide you with the necessary resources and tips to succeed.

Preparation Tips

  • Understand the placement process and timeline.
  • Prepare a strong resume highlighting your skills and achievements.
  • Practice aptitude tests and technical interviews.
  • Work on your communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies.

Resources

  • Online courses and tutorials
  • Mock interviews and group discussions
  • Placement preparation books and guides
  • Company-specific interview experiences
  • Professional networking platforms
